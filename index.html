Cash

Cash payments are the standard recommended method, and will give your project the highest priority.

Equity

Payment in equity requires a business plan, and a longer negotiating process prior to starting the work. Recommended only for start-ups that are strapped for cash.

Research Co-Authorships

Can provide a discounted rate for resesarch institutions. Requires a research plan of the publication, and a list of targetted journals.

Pats

For poor social/environmental, not for profit orginisations only. Our team devotes one day per week for pro-bono work, and your project will have the lowest priority.
